Beef Stew

Description

Simple Instant pot Beef Stew recipe.

Prep Time: 10-15 minutes Cook Time: ~ 2 hours

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef chuck, cubed
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/2 tsp thyme
  • 1 cup baby carrots, sliced
  • 1 cup celery, sliced
  • 5-6 small red potatoes, cubed
  • 1 bag of frozen peas
  • Oil
  • Beef boullion (start with 2 tsp and add as needed)
  • Onion Powder
  • 1 can golden mushroom soup
  • 1 bag of lipton beefy onion
  • 2 tbsp worchester sauce
  • 1 tbsp cornstartch
  • FLour

Instructions

  1. Season meat with salt, pepper, garlic powder and onion powder. Coat with flour then brown/sear all sides of meat in pot with oil on high heat.
  2. Remove meat from pan. Add in mushroom, onion and garlic, then saute for 3 3 minutes. Add in wine to deglaze; Scrape the bottom of pot to mix flavors
  3. Add meat back in, along with tomato paste, bay leaves and thyme.
  4. Add beefy onion mix, 1 can of golden mushroom soup, beef boullion, and enough water to cover the stew.
  5. Stir then add in carrots, celery and potatoes. Simmer on low heat for 1.5 hours.
  6. Add frozen peas, season to taste and cook for another 5 minutes.
  7. Remove bay leaves and serve.
